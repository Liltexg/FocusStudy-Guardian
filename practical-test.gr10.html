Practical Test - Grade 10

Instructions:

  • Complete the tasks using Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Word as described below.
  • Write your answers in the provided fields.
  • Click on a question to reveal the answer field.
  • Ensure to save your work in the respective applications and submit the files.

Microsoft Excel Tasks

  1. Create a spreadsheet to track monthly expenses. Include columns for Date, Description, Category, Amount, and Total. Use formulas to calculate the total amount for each category and the grand total for the month.

    2. Memo: Your spreadsheet should include columns for Date, Description, Category, Amount, and Total. Use SUM formulas to calculate totals and ensure proper formatting for ease of use.

  2. Design a chart that visualizes the data from the monthly expenses spreadsheet. Choose an appropriate chart type and make sure it clearly represents the data.

    3. Memo: The chart should be designed to represent the data clearly, such as a pie chart for category distribution or a bar chart for monthly comparisons. Ensure it is well-labeled and easy to understand.

  3. Use conditional formatting to highlight expenses over a certain amount in your spreadsheet. Describe the steps you took to apply this formatting.

    4. Memo: To apply conditional formatting, select the cells in the Amount column, go to Conditional Formatting, and set up a rule to highlight cells greater than a specified amount with a color of your choice.

    4. Memo: To perform a mail merge, create a document with placeholders for personalized information, then use the Mail Merge feature to connect to a contact list and merge data into the document.
